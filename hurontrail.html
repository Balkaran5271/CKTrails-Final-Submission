Huron Trail and Forest loop

Huron Trail and Forest Trail Loop is a 2.7 kilometer moderately trafficked loop trail located near Kitchener, Ontario, Canada that features a lake and is good for all skill levels. The trail offers a number of activity options and is best used from May until November.
