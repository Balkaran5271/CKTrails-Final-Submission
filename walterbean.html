Walter Bean Grand River Trail

Walter Bean Grand River Trail is a 17.5 kilometer out and back trail located near Kitchener, Ontario, Canada that features a river and is rated as moderate. The trail is primarily used for hiking and walking. Dogs are also able to use this trail but must be kept on leash.
